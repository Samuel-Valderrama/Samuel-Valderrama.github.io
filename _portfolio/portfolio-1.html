The Automobile Guide Vehicle was designed to transport products, withdraw them and place them autonomously, intended to be used in warehouses.

For the development and simulation of this AGV, I had the opportunity to be the project leader where I was able to coordinate the mechanical design and program the embedded systems to perform various robot functionalities such as movements, obstacle detection, communication with a mobile app, implementation of protocols UART, I2C, among others.

For the AGV functionality, an ARM-type microcontroller was programmed in C language, an open-loop control was implemented for the movement in MATLAB, and we used SolidWorks to simulate the mechanical design.
